ABOUT US

Triprakara is a non-profit organization that holds teaching community in Bandung. Through this website teaching community and the general public can share stories and reports related activities. Triprakara will be a bridge for them to submit a report on the data that are not school children in Bandung to the government. By utilizing Triprakara it will show the latest data and the latest news from the teaching community and the general public.

Triprakara focus on children's education, because according to the legislation regard about children and education are: Republic of Indonesia Law Number 23 year 2002 on Child Protection that the government shall be required to provide a minimum of nine (9) years basic education for all childre. And Republic of Indonesia Law Number 39 year 1999 about Human Rights, that every child has the right to access to education and schooling as befits his interests, talents, and intellectual capacity.

Harapan Bangsa Bina Budaya Bangsa

Harapan Bangsa Bina Budaya Bangsa merupakan sebuah komunitas yang berdiri di Jl. Kopo No.21 Bandung. Komunitas ini berdiri sejak Tahun 2014.
